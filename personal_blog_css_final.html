Based on CivCLicker, I wanted to make my own version of an incremental clicker game.
It's been fun making it, having fun with the design and figuring out the mechanics of the game and how to make them in way that feels rewarding when playing.
It is by far the harder thing I've tried to do.
This is the first time I’ve had to manage multiple systems at once that all interact this much with one another : population, production, building requirements...
I'm still working on it, and it's probably going to take a while !
